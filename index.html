CRUELTY TO ANIMALS

In 2018, US government statistics put the number of laboratory animals used in research at 780,070, an increase of 5.7% from 2017.
This includes both public and private institutions. These statistics do not include all animals as most mice, rats, and fish are not covered by the Animal Welfare Act – though they are
still covered by other regulations that protect animal welfare.
We also have not included the 122,717 animals which were kept in research facilities in 2018 but were not involved in any research studies.












WHAT CAN WE DO?

5 easy ways you can prevent animal cruelty

1.Report animal abuse if you see it.
2. Understand the link between animal cruelty and domestic abuse.
3. Teach children to respect animals.
4. Volunteer to help animals.
5. Support legislation that promotes kindness to animals.
